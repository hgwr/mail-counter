Privacy Policy - mail-counter Safari Extension

Last updated: March 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to mail-counter Safari Extension (the "Extension"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your data.

2. Data Access and Usage

The extension retrieves the unread email count from your currently open Gmail tab in Safari and displays it as a badge on the Safari toolbar. It does not access, store, or transmit the content of your emails. All data processing occurs locally on your device, and no data is shared with any third parties.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The Extension does not share, transfer, or disclose any user data to third parties. All data processing occurs locally within your browser.

4. Data Protection Measures

The Extension operates exclusively within the Safari browser and does not store, log, or transmit any sensitive data. Data extracted (unread count) never leaves your device.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

The Extension does not store any user data. Any information used (such as the unread email count) is temporary and exists only for displaying a badge within your Safari browser.
